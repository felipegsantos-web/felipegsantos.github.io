Consultancy

Evidence-based policy advice for international organisations, NGOs, and research institutions.

I offer consultancy rooted in over a decade of empirical research on political participation, social movements, far-right politics, and democratic change in Europe. My clients have included the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), the European Climate Foundation, and local NGOs in Spain, Slovakia, and Hungary. I work with organisations that need rigorous, independent analysis to inform programme design, evaluation, or policy.

What I Offer

Substantive Policy Advice

Expert analysis on political and social dynamics relevant to programme design, evaluation, and advocacy.

Social Movements & Civil Society

Analysis of collective action, protest repertoires, civil society mobilisation, and the conditions under which movements achieve policy change.

Far-Right Politics & Democratic Backsliding

Research-based analysis of far-right parties, strategies, and the erosion of democratic institutions in Spain and Europe — relevant for risk assessment, programme design, and counter-strategy.

Political Participation & Civic Engagement

Expert input on citizen engagement, voter behaviour, and the relationship between institutional and extra-institutional politics.

Methods & Statistics

Advisory and technical support on quantitative and qualitative research design for organisations conducting or commissioning research.

Survey Design & Experiments

Design and analysis of surveys and survey experiments on political attitudes, behaviour, and participation. Methodological advice for organisations planning fieldwork.

Statistical Analysis

Statistical modelling and data analysis for policy research, impact evaluation, and monitoring and evaluation (M&E) frameworks.

Qualitative & Mixed Methods

Expert guidance on ethnographic, interview-based, and mixed-methods approaches — including how to combine qualitative and quantitative evidence for policy purposes.

Approach

I combine academic rigour with practical clarity. My work is grounded in peer-reviewed research and translated into actionable insights for non-specialist audiences. I am experienced in working with international organisations operating in multilingual, cross-national contexts.

Work With Me

I welcome enquiries from international organisations, NGOs, EU and UN institutions, and research bodies seeking independent expert analysis.
