Felipe G. Santos

Research Fellow · Political Sociology · City St George's, University of London, United Kingdom

I study social movements, political participation, far-right politics, and democratic change in Europe. My work spans ethnography, surveys, and comparative methods — from housing movements and civil society to democratic backsliding and protest repertoires. Investigo movimientos sociales, participación política, extrema derecha y cambio democrático en Europa.
